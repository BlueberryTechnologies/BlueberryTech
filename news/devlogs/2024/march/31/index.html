We have decided to condense the site down to what is really important. From now on, all of the content you will see from us is what is important, no bullshit, no filters, no nothing.

We as a company are going to try and focus on what is really important, learning and improving on our skills. We will continue to learn new ways to approach everything and make sure we are striving to be the best. If that means sacrificing a 'good looking' website then thats fine with me.
